WELCOME TO My DashBoard

An interactive open source software tool for rapid analysis for your business.

My DashBoard enables intuitive, fast and reproducible analysis of your business without prior software experience. It makes it easy to understand and communicate important trends, performance and results of your products. Check out many of our other solutions here: https://youneed.us.com/#solutions

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH My DashBoard:

  • Search for products, analyze trends, quickly review your business performance and/or strategically filter by various groups.
  • Download databases, tables, charts, graphs and statistics relevant to your business for easy distribution or presentation. 
  • Analyze performance and compare growth targets set for your business. 
  • Identify areas of the business that could be leveraged and made more profitable. by strategic intervention.
